How It Works

Enter your summoner name and region above to generate recommendations for champions to play. The Recommender compares your public summoner profile to thousands of other players and finds people who perform similarly on the champions you play. Then it assumes you'll be good (or bad) at the same champions they are and reports the top and bottom champions.

It doesn't filter out champions you already play or care how often you play a champion. That means that there could be (and and probably will be) champions on your list that you've never played before.
